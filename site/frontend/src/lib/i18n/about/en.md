# About
## Introduction
This service calculates and provides skill ratings for Taiko no Tatsujin players based on competition records from [Donderhiroba](https://donderhiroba.jp).

## How to Participate
Participate in the Donderhiroba competitions listed on the [main page](/), and your ratings will be automatically calculated after the session ends.

## Rating Calculation Logic
Your rating is updated using the Glicko-2 algorithm based on your performance against 20 players with similar ratings who participated in the same session.
